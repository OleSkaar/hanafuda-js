Hanafuda

This web app helps you count points in the Japanese card game Hanafuda. Tap the cards from the deck at the bottom, and your score will automatically update. There is an undo button in the top right if you hit the wrong card. Your hand will be saved in the browser - to start a new game, hit the new button. Finally, for a more detailed view of the cards in your hand, hit the expand button. Enjoy!
